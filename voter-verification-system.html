How It Works

A simple, secure process from start to finish

Voter Arrival

Voter arrives at the polling station and proceeds to the verification counter equipped with our system.

Identity Scanning

Election official scans voter ID card or QR code from voter registration app to initiate verification.

Biometric Confirmation

System prompts for fingerprint or facial recognition to confirm identity with the registered data.

Eligibility Check

System instantly cross-references with the electoral database to confirm eligibility and voting status.

Verification Complete

Upon successful verification, system generates a secure token and authorizes the voter to proceed.

Cast Vote

Voter proceeds to cast their vote while the system logs the verification (not the vote) for audit purposes.
